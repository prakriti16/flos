About FLOS

FLOS is an initiative, a mission, and a commitment to science and life by the curious, for the curious. FLOS is a multi-platform science media company built to showcase science in life and life in science. In a world of total information, what one might grasp may not be enough. Resultingly, one's accomplishments are mostly superficial: "knowing the name", as Dr. Feynman would call it. But we, at FLOS, believe that you deserve more than just knowing the name to fully experience the beauty of life. So, we share and recommend interesting science every week to enlighten you about advancements in the world. And as time passes, we assure you that you will find yourself in a much more comfortable position while dealing with "sciency" stuff.
